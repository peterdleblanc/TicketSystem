On many computers you may need to set the Local intranet settings to low..
Local Intranet to Low
  1. Open an new internet explorer window.
  2. Goto Tools > Internet Options.
  3. Goto the Security tab.
  4. From the "Web content zone" section choose "Local intranet".
  5. Click the button "Default Level".
  6. Move the slider from "Medium-low" down to "Low".
  7. Click OK.
